Would you rather roll for abilities or point buy?
Point buy:
- how many points do you have?
- determine ability scores

Roll for abilities: 
- standard: roll 4d6, discard the lowest number, and add the remaining three together. 
- repeat until 6 numbers are generated
- assign numbers to ability scores
